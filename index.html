Age Rating 13+

Emak Crate is designed for users aged 13 and older. The game contains moderately challenging puzzle elements that are best enjoyed by players who are 13 years of age or older.

Absolute Privacy

Your privacy is highly valued. The Emak Crate application operates entirely offline and does not collect, store, or share any personal data, analytics, or device information.

PICO-8 Engine

Built on the PICO-8 fantasy console, featuring custom level designs made with Tiled, and packaged with Flutter for a smooth mobile experience.
