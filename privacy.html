Zero Data Collection & Age Limitation

The Emak Crate application is built with complete privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app is rated for ages 13 and older.

Welcome to Emak Crate. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with our mobile application.

Our application is intended for users who are at least 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly target or collect information from children under 13.

We do not collect any personal data, usage statistics, analytics, or device identifiers. The application operates entirely offline on your mobile device. Because of this:
